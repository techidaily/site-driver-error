### Method 1: Check your Blue Yeti microphone connection

 Your Blue Yeti microphone may not be connected properly to your computer. It’s worth checking your microphone connection.

1. Try reconnecting your microphone and see if this helps you.
2. Try another USB port on your computer, then check to see if this fixes your driver issue.
