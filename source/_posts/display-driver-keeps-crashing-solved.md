### Fix 2: Uninstall and reinstall your display driver

 This problem may also happen if the current display driver on your computer is corrupt. So you should remove the driver first and reinstall it again to see if it helps.

Here’s how to uninstall and reinstall your display driver:

 1) On your keyboard, press **the Windows logo key** and **R**  at the same time, then type **devmgmt.msc** and press **Enter** .

### Fix 3: Roll back your display driver
